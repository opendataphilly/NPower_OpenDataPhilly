  • Congratulations to the winners of the OpenDataRace!
    We are excited about the huge number of people who supported the contest! Thank you for becoming involved in the process of open government through the release of data.
    So what's next? The groups that nominated the three winning data sets will receive cash prizes of $2000, $1000, and $500. The OpenDataPhilly team will work with the organizations who hold the requested data to attempt to make it available for public use. We will also be hosting hack-a-thon events in the spring to create applications that use the data. Most importantly, we will continue to promote open data in Philadelphia and we hope you will join us in this effort!
    Visit OpenDataPhilly.org and continue to support the open data community!
